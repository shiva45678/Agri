About Us

Welcome to Newtons Agriculture. Based in the heart of Andhra Pradesh's agricultural belt, we are blessed with the fertile lands nurtured by the Krishna River.

Located in Veladikothapalem (521182), our mission is to blend traditional farming wisdom with modern agricultural science. We believe in sustainable growth, empowering local farmers, and producing yields that are both abundant and eco-friendly.

Whether you are looking for premium seeds, organic fertilizers, or expert farming consultations, Newtons Agriculture is your trusted local partner. Let's grow a healthier tomorrow, together.
